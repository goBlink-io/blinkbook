## Welcome to goBlink

goBlink is a cross-chain crypto transfer and payment platform that makes it easy to send, receive, and accept cryptocurrency across 12 supported blockchains. Powered by 1Click and NEAR Intents, goBlink handles the complexity of cross-chain swaps so you don't have to.

## For Users

Learn how to send your first cross-chain transfer, create payment links, and use the goBlink Telegram bot.

- **Getting Started** — Send your first transfer in under a minute
- **How Transfers Work** — Understand the 1Click cross-chain routing
- **Supported Chains** — See all 12 supported chains and tokens
- **Payment Links** — Create shareable payment links
- **Telegram Bot** — Transfer crypto directly from Telegram
